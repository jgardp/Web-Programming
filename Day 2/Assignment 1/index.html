Dune: Part Two

Dune is based off the epic sci-fi novel written by Frank Herbert.

"May thy knife chip and shatter" - Atreides


Characters:

  • Paul Atreides: Duke of House Atreides who aims to regain control over Arrakis.
  • Chani: Fremen who teaches Paul about Arrakis and is his lover.
  • Stilgar: Leader of the Fremen on the planet Arrakis.
  • Vladimir Harkonnen: The Baron from Giedi Prime who takes over Arrakis from House Atreides.
  • Feyd-Rautha: Vladimir's nephew and heir who is trained in battle.
  • Emperor Shaddam IV: The current Emperor of the Padishah Empire.
